**Front end** 

**All behaviors come from explicit state**

**Semantic state over animation names**

**Continuous change must be monotonic & explainable**

**Rendering is a pure function of state**

**Prefer minimal abstractions**

**Tests verify behavior, not implementation**



**back end**

**Article 0 — Separation of Responsibility & Explainability First**

**Explanation:在任何后端代码设计、修改或 Review 中，系统必须优先满足：**

**（1）职责分离可追溯（Separation of Responsibility）**
 **（2）数据与控制流的显式可解释性（Explicit Explainability）**
